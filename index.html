Technique, reading and musical freedom.

About

Actor, pianist and educator based in Bucharest.

Radu Mateucă graduated from both the BA and MA Acting programmes at UNATC Bucharest. His screen work includes The Day of the Jackal, Doamna Flo, Ecce Homo Brâncoveanu, Tata mută munții, Vlad and Brigada Nimic; his theatre credits include Marfă Vie, Toxic, Viața lui Ciprian Porumbescu, Taina Paștelui and History Boys. He has played piano since the age of six and currently teaches piano and Romanian to international students.
